Random Ideas

The Idea

I currently have a general idea of what I want this game to be, but am still fuzzy on the exact details. The idea is a game somewhat in between Hades and Magic Survival in concept, where the world is seperated into rooms which are either procedurally generated. or premade and randomly taped together. The general theme of the game is to have a Sci-fi thematic, where the player is on a space station and was hired to clean it out after an accident.

The plans

What I have already done

So far, I have made a lot of progress on the physics engine, and the basic gun weapon system. Bullets shoot with the correct attributes and do damage. Popups appear properly and everything is happy, though the game lacks almost all content.

What I plan on doing

The immediate task is to make melee combat at least sort of work. After that, I want to design a small test room prefab and try to get multiple rooms to appear together. Graphics and sprites and the like are very low on priority.
